    Global Logistics & Safe Exporting Operations

    By Operations Team May 25, 2026 0 Comments

    Shipping high-density carbon products across multiple continents requires strict planning, quality control, and secure shipping partnerships. At Coalmade, we have designed an end-to-end logistics pipeline that ensures safe, reliable, and on-time global cargo shipments.

    Custom-Engineered Secure Packaging

    One of the greatest challenges in bulk exporting is protecting materials from moisture, friction, and heat during weeks at sea. Excess humidity can degrade quality, while extreme heat poses combustion risks in cargo holds.

    "Secure exporting isn't just about loading container boxes; it is about controlling the environment from the factory floor all the way to the destination harbor."

    To prevent these issues, our export products are packed in custom multi-layer, UV-resistant, and moisture-proof poly woven bags. These high-strength packaging layers insulate the carbon and keep the product completely dry, ensuring it arrives in perfect condition.

    Rigorous Drop & Breakage Testing

    Before any batch is cleared for global shipment, it must pass our drop and compression test protocols. Briquettes are dropped repeatedly from heights of over 2 meters onto steel plates. This ensures they maintain structural strength and do not crumble during transport, giving our clients a dust-free experience upon arrival.

    By maintaining partnerships with top-tier international shipping lines, we guarantee full tracking transparency, customs clearing support, and seamless delivery to major international ports across Europe, the Americas, and Asia.
