    Steam Coal

    Our steam coal is optimally sized and washed for maximum thermal efficiency. Ideal for power plants and industrial boilers, it offers a high calorific value, low ash content, and consistent moisture levels — ensuring stable and efficient combustion in every shipment.

    Coking Coal

    Sourced from premium deposits, our coking coal meets the strict metallurgical requirements of steel production. With high carbon content, low sulfur, and excellent coke strength after reaction (CSR), it is the preferred choice for blast furnace and DRI steel mills.

    Lignite Coal

    Our lignite coal is a cost-effective energy source for mine-mouth power stations and cement kilns. Delivered in uniform sizing with reliable moisture control, it provides a lower-emission alternative for large-volume industrial consumers seeking operational economy.

    The highest grade of coal we supply, our anthracite features carbon content exceeding 90%, near-zero smoke output, and exceptional heat density. Widely used in filtration, electrode manufacturing, and heating applications where purity and performance are non-negotiable.

    Shipping and Export

    We provide reliable and efficient global shipping and export services, ensuring our products reach customers safely and on time. With trusted logistics partners and a focus on quality assurance, we make international trade smooth and hassle-free.

    Coal Sizing & Grading

    We supply coal in a full range of standardized sizes — from lump and nut to pea and fines — precisely graded to match the technical requirements of each customer. Our on-site screening and crushing facilities allow us to customize output size distributions to align with your boiler, furnace, or kiln specifications, ensuring optimal feed rates and minimal waste.

    Global Shipping & Logistics

    Coalmade operates a robust international export network, handling shipments via bulk vessels, barges, and containerized cargo. We manage all freight documentation, customs clearance, and port coordination — offering CIF, FOB, and CFR terms. Our partnerships with leading shipping lines ensure on-time delivery to ports across Asia, the Middle East, and beyond.

    Secure Packaging Standards

    Each coal product is packaged to international trade standards, protecting quality from origin to destination. We offer bulk loose loading for large shipments and big-bag or jumbo-sack options for specialty grades. Moisture-resistant liners and sealed containers prevent contamination and weight loss during long-haul voyages, preserving the quality you purchased.

    Combustion & Drop Shatter Test

    Every batch undergoes rigorous combustion trials and drop shatter tests before dispatch. Combustion analysis verifies calorific value, flame behavior, and emissions profile. The drop shatter test evaluates mechanical durability, ensuring coal maintains its graded size through handling and transport — reducing fines generation and guaranteeing the specification your operation depends on.
